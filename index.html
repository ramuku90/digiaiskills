Our Story

Founded in 2023, DigiAISkills was born from a simple observation: traditional education wasn't keeping pace with the rapidly evolving tech industry. Our founders, a team of AI researchers and education experts, saw an opportunity to leverage artificial intelligence to create a more adaptive, efficient, and engaging learning experience.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of students worldwide, helping them transform their careers and achieve their dreams in technology. Our AI-powered platform has helped students land jobs at top tech companies, launch successful startups, and become leaders in their fields.
